Enhanced Conversation Sample Demo

This app represents a fictional cognitive car. Commands issued to the car will be answered by the Conversation service. For example, "Turn on my wipers" or "Turn up the radio". It is also possible to ask detailed questions about how to operate a car - details can be found in this car manual. These will be answered by the Retrieve and Rank service. For example, "How do I check my tire pressure" or "How do I turn on cruise control?" The app's responses are shown on the left while the right shows the detailed JSON responses from the server.
